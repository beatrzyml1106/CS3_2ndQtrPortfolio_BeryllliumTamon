Olivia Rodrigo

Olivia Rodrigo is a well-known singer, songwriter, and actress. Her fame kicked off from her acting debut in 2015 and grew rapidly ever since. She is renowned for her debut album "SOUR" in 2021 with many top hits such as Driver's License and Deja Vu. later, in 2023, she released her second album "GUTS" which was also a hit for the fans. Being a rookie in the singing industry, she does not have a large discography yet each song is an excellent showcase of her talents. Many fans anticipate for a new album and world tour.
